Maya’s 12-week Endurance Transformation

Maya entered the program unable to run more than 2 minutes continuously. The plan combined progressive intervals, strength maintenance and recovery. Here's the full timeline:

  1. Weeks 1-4: Build base with walk-run intervals and core stability.
  2. Weeks 5-8: Increase run intervals, add threshold work, maintain strength.
  3. Weeks 9-12: Consolidation, race prep and pacing strategy.

Outcome: Completed 5K, improved VO₂, and better sleep quality.
