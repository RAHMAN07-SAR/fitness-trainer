Our Story

Founded in 2015, NextGen Fitness was built on a mission to transform how people approach health and fitness. We believe in balance — where physical fitness, mental well-being, and community come together.

From small beginnings, we have grown into a thriving fitness hub, trusted by thousands of members across Bengaluru and beyond.
